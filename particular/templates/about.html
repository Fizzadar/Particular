Particular is a search engine that only indexes a small slice of the web, as chosen by it's users.

Websites are voted for inclusion in the index by users. Anyone can become a user and contribute votes and websites. All websites we crawl must conform to some reasonable rules regarding well built websites. Once these have been verified, and sufficient votes gained, a site will be added to the index and crawled.
